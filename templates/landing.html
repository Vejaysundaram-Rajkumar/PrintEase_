About Us


PrintEase: Streamlined Hassle Free Printing service for REC Students!



Our aim is to streamline the process of searching for and ordering printing services from local printer shops. Progress updates are shared with the customer, and upon completion, the customer is notified to come and collect their printouts. This application aims to make the process of ordering and receiving printing services more efficient and convenient for both customers and printer shops.

The application allows users to send documents to a connected printer and make payments for the printing service directly from their mobile devices. The system is designed to streamline the printing process, making it more convenient and efficient for users. The application uses a secure connection to communicate with the printer, ensuring that the user's sensitive information is protected.
